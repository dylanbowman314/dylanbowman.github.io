This is an addendum to my home page. I'm writing this to be of particular use to people who work with me or would like to work with me.

Overview

I'm a technical generalist with experience as a software engineer (and manager), deep learning researcher, and founder. I've been at my best when I've managed a team of 5 ICs or fewer and set the high-level strategy for the team. I tend to write obsessively on the job and in general I think I am a clear and effective communicator. I also try my best to be automatically strategic.

You might not enjoy working with me if you are expecting a 99th percentile engineer or researcher. I am a reasonable IC but sadly I am spiritually non-technical and generally enjoy people more than things. I don't interface well with large, complex systems and complement well with engineers who do excel at this kind of work.

I also tend to be aesthetically opinionated: briefly, I think most people try to do too much and you should really aim for foundationally-sound designs that never conflict with function. If you're going for a splash, make it the centerpiece and surround it with solid, classic, time-tested auxiliary items. I can really dig my heels in on this, as many prior coworkers can attest. I don't want to make this its own blog post but most startup landing pages look as good as their founders dress.

One last weird note is that I am vegetarian and get a lot of joy from working and eating together with other vegetarians, although I am not vegetarian for strictly moral reasons (link placeholder for a blog post I'll write later).
